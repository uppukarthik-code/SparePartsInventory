# Monthly Dead Stock Review
*Southern Railway Signalling & Telecom Spare Parts — 07 June 2026*

## Executive Summary

There are 190 dead-stock items in the depots worth Rs 7.63 crore, which is 14.9% of operational inventory value. Of these, 190 items have had no movement for more than two years. This is capital and shelf space locked in spares that are not being issued and may be deteriorating or turning obsolete. The largest items by value are listed below and should be physically verified, then reviewed for reuse elsewhere on the division, transfer to a depot that needs them, or disposal under extant rules. Clearing this stock recovers space and, where disposal yields scrap value, returns some capital. Management is asked to convene a dead-stock board to act on the top items this financial year.
